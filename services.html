Services

Our downtown shop can be booked for suitable events, and have been configured for lectures, movie screenings, and dance parties. Rates vary depending on the nature of event and how much setup support you need.

Venue Information

Our downtown shop is located at 10612-105 Avenue immediately north of MacEwan University. It has excellent transit and bike network access. Maximum occupancy is 48 people. Chairs, tables, a projection screen, and other useful equipment is available if needed. Note: we have only one partly accessible washroom.
